Our Mission

Developing clean energy technologies that align with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

Company Profile

Dwi Angkasa Gita is a leading innovator in the clean energy sector, dedicated to developing sustainable, renewable, and clean energy technologies. Our core mission is to contribute to a greener future by creating technologies that support the global Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

Our primary focus lies in the research and development of fundamental and supporting technologies for clean, new, and renewable energy. We aim to enable high-efficiency, sustainable, and resilient energy solutions, contributing to the transformation of the energy landscape.

Our Values

  • Innovation: Continuously advancing our technology to meet the growing demand for clean energy.
  • Sustainability: Committed to environmental stewardship through technology that minimizes carbon footprint.
  • Collaboration: Working with industry partners and communities to create a broader positive impact.
